EEG Signal Processing Headstage

In this project, I designed a headstage for EEG signal processing. The headstage is responsible for amplifying and filtering the EEG signals before they are sent to the main processing unit. The headstage is designed to be low-noise and high-bandwidth to ensure the highest quality signal is sent to the processing unit.

Castor Digital: Tech Solutions for Small Business

During the COVID-19 pandemic, I recognized the importance of online business, and created a website for my family's restaurant. After finishing the site, I found myself helping more and more of my local businesses with web development, digital marketing, customized software and many of their technical solutions. Working with my friends and colleagues, I started Castor Digital, my small business dedicated to helping other businesses with their technical needs.

In 2022, I plan to expand Castor Digital and provide more with customized software for assets and inventory management, and integrated blockchain projects.
