Projects

EEG Signal Processing Headstage

In the Spring of 2019, I constructed an Electroencephalogram (EEG) Headpiece with a 3D-printed frame and a kit from OpenBCI. This project was done in an effort to study basic signal processing and learn more about how the brain works. After completing the headpiece, I brought it to an outreach event at Serviam Girls Academy to teach and inspire the young students about neuroscience and electrical engineering.

Robotics Software Engineering

From 2019 to 2020 I was a member on the team at TRIC Robotics; a startup company focusing on alleviating pesticides for strawberries using UVC light and autonomous vehicle robots. At TRIC, I've worked on multiple small projects for the robot platform, learning to develop better software for data collection and analysis.

TerminalTots: NFT Collection on the Polygon Blockchain

In the Fall of 2021, for a fun project to get into NFTs (Non-fungible Tokens) I created a collection of randomly-generated icons based on retro computers/"terminals". Each Terminal Tot is minted on the Polygon blockchain and live for sale on OpenSea marketplace.
